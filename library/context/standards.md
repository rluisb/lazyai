# Context: Standards

**Category:** Process
**Status:** Active

---

## Rule

Adhere to all established standards.

## Rationale

Ensures consistency, quality, and traceability across the codebase.

## Guidelines

- **Code Standards:** Follow the language-specific style guide.
- **Testing Standards:** Write unit, integration, and E2E tests.
- **Security Standards:** Follow secure coding practices.
- **Process Standards:** Follow the standard development workflow.

## Enforcement

- PR review checklist
- CI gates for tests and linting
