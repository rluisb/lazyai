# Knowledge Map

**Category:** Documentation
**Status:** Active

---

## Rule

Maintain an accurate and up-to-date knowledge map.

## Rationale

Ensures knowledge is captured and shared across the team.

## Guidelines

- **Map Knowledge:** Identify key knowledge areas and their owners.
- **Update Map:** Update the map as knowledge areas evolve.
- **Share Map:** Share the map with the team.

## Enforcement

- PR review checklist
- CI gates for tests and linting
